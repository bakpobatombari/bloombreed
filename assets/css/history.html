OUR HISTORY

Honouring our heritage. Inspiring Bright Futures

Established in 1998, Bloombreed Schools has grown from a burgeoning nursery and primary school into a vibrant co-educational institution with both Day and Boarding High Schools. Our successful evolution has placed us in a distinct position to offer children a smooth and stable transition in their academic journey as well as assuage every parent’s search for quality education.
Bloombreed Schools is the brainchild of Mrs. Olufunke Sunmonu, fondly called Big Auntie – a seasoned educationist, wife and mother with strong passion for quality education and the desire to inculcate the fear of God in children which inevitably transforms them into exemplary leaders.
The Chairman of the Board, Sir Mutiu Sunmonu (CON), has chaired lots of innovative, transformative and top tier programmes in the school.
From inception in 2007, the Boarding High School located at Boskel Road, off Aba Expressway, Port Harcourt, has committed itself to the provision of academic excellence, quality al life, physical-mental synergy and spiritual-moral conduct that will position learners to compete with their peers globally. We take pride in our superior infrastructure as well as our peaceful and secure environment.
